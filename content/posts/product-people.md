## What it is
A series of long-form profiles on team members.

## What I was trying to do
Most company profiles read like extended LinkedIn summaries. I wanted to move away from that and write pieces that people would read even if they didn’t care about the company.
The focus was on small, telling details. Not just what someone does, but how they think, what they obsess over, what they’re quietly proud of.
