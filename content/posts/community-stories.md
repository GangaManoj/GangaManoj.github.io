## What it is
Profiles of community members including builders, founders, and contributors.

## What I was trying to do
This was less about the company and more about the ecosystem around it. The challenge here was distance. These were not people I worked with every day, so the writing had to do more work in building connection.
I leaned into narrative arcs. Origin stories, turning points, quiet moments of conviction. The idea was to make each piece feel like a story you’d want to finish.
