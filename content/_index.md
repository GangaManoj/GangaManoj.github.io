I’m interested in people, and the things they don’t always say out loud.

Sometimes I write about them. Other times, I help companies understand them.

My work sits somewhere between storytelling and strategy. It usually comes down to figuring out what’s worth saying, and how to say it so someone actually reads it. Sometimes that looks like profiles. Sometimes it looks like narrative pieces. Once, it looked like a satirical newspaper on tech culture.

This is a selection of that work.
